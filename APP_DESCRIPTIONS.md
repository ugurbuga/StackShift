# StackShift App Store Descriptions

## English (default)

### Short Description
Reverse-Tetris inspired puzzle game. Drag, snap, and clear rows for combos!

### Full Description
StackShift is a unique puzzle game inspired by Tetris, but with a twist: instead of falling from the top, pieces spawn near the bottom. Drag pieces into place, snap them to the grid, and clear completed rows for points, combos, and increasing pressure. Enjoy animated feedback and special block types. Customize your theme, play with touch or mouse, and challenge yourself to master the board!
